Q1. Temperature Tracker
Problem:
You're building a weather station app. The temperature is recorded every hour in a tuple (immutable). Your job is to calculate:
Highest temperature
Lowest temperature
Average temperature

Input Example:
temps = (31.2, 30.8, 32.1, 35.6, 34.2, 33.5)

Output:
Highest: 35.6  
Lowest: 30.8  
Average: 32.9
